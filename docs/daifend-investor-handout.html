Why now

  • AI systems are becoming autonomous and stateful (memory + tools).
  • Attackers target meaning: poisoning, injection, manipulation, identity spoofing.
  • Enterprises need runtime integrity + recovery, not dashboards.

What Daifend does

  • Detect: signatures + drift correlation across memory/RAG/agents.
  • Enforce: policy gates on writes, retrieval, and tool execution.
  • Heal: isolate blast radius, rollback to trusted baselines, restore trust.
